### Why it matters
Conventional rectangular microfluidic models do not faitfully recapitulate physilogy of human vasculature, partly hampering their adaptation and application. 

### What I contributed
Developing an autologous iPSC-derived small-airway-on-chip integrating donor-matched epithelial, endothelial,
        stromal, and immune components. Using matched disomic vs trisomic systems to study baseline immune hyper-responsiveness
        and exaggerated inflammatory/viral responses, enabling therapeutic discovery pipelines.

### Methods
- Microfabrication
- Perfusion control
- ECM design
